## Liberal Arts Requirements:

- BIOL 1000, Principles of Modern Biology, is a Scientific Ways of Knowing course in the Liberal Arts Program.
- MATH 1100, Elementary Mathematics - Numeration Systems, is a Quantitative Reasoning course in the Liberal Arts Program.
- EDUC 3120, Educational Assessment for the Classroom Teacher, is a Writing Intensive course in the Liberal Arts Program.
- EDUC 4120, Organization and Management for the Elementary Classroom, is a Writing Intensive course in the Liberal Arts Program.
- ENGL 1400, Valuing Through Literature, is an Aesthetic Ways of Knowing course in the Liberal Arts Program.
- HIST 2000, History of World Civilization, is a Civic Ways of Knowing course in the Liberal Arts Program.
- HIST 2110, American Civilization I, is a Civic Ways of Knowing course in the Liberal Arts Program.
- MUED 2110, Music for the Elementary Classroom, is an Aesthetic Ways of Knowing course in the Liberal Arts Program.
- Speaking Intensive course approved by Dean of Education.
