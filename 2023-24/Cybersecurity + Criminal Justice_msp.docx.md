## Cybersecurity Major + Criminal Justice Major

120 total credit hours

Cybersecurity majors add + 31 credit hours 2 LA required courses to earn Criminal Justice major, or

Criminal Justice majors add + 54 credit hours -2 LA required courses to earn Cybersecurity BA Note: All requirements for both majors must be met, as well as requirements for the Liberal Arts Core Curriculum.
