## Christian Ministries Major (46 credit hours)

2025-2026\_\_\_\_\_\_

The Christian Ministries major is designed to prepare students to serve as Christian leaders and servants in the world.  It offers undergraduate study of ministry which may lead to:

1. Lay ministry while pursuing another profession.
2. A position of leadership in ministry in a local congregation, a church-related organization, or a related field.
3. Graduate study in ministry.  This major is intended to be a first step in a plan of lifelong learning for ministry.

## Major Requirements:

## Departmental Core (18 hours):

- BIBL 2000, Introduction to the Bible, 3 credit hours (offered Fall 2025 and Spring 2026)
- BIBL 2050, Methods in Biblical Exegesis, 3 credit hours** (offered Fall 2025)
- RLGN 2000, Introduction to Religion, 3 credit hours  (not offered 2025-26)
- RLGN 2130, Introduction to Christian Ethics, 3 credit hours**  (offered 2026-27)
- RLGN 3040, Introduction to Christian Theology, 3 credit hours**  (offered Spring 2026)
- RLGN 3060, History of Christianity, 3 credit hours  (offered Fall 2025)

## Ministry Core (15 hours):

- CMIN 2000, Introduction to Church Ministry, 3 credit hours  (offered Fall 2025)
- CMIN 3050, Homiletics, 3 credit hours*** (offered in person in 2026-2027)
- CMIN 3080, Pastoral Care and Counseling, 3 credit hours***  (offered Fall 2025)
- CMIN 3910, Ministry Seminar***  (offered Fall 2025)
- CMIN 4250, Theology in a Ministry Context, 3 credit hours***  (offered Spring 2026)

## Internship/experiential courses (4 hours):

- CMIN 2810 Ministry Exploration, 1 credit hour + CMIN 3340, The Community of Faith in an Urban Culture, 3 credit hours (instructor's consent required - offered most terms, summer session includes travel to Atlanta), OR
- CMIN 4860, Practicum in Ministry, 3 credit hours*** (offered most terms), OR
- CMIN 2810 Ministry Exploration + MUSC 3800, Internship in Church Music, 3 credit hours (offered Fall 2025 and Spring 2026)

Nine  (9)  additional  hours  from  any  courses  offered  by  the  department  (BIBL,  CMIN,  PACT,  PHIL,  or  RLGN captions) or MUSC 3150 (not offered 2025-26) , 3160 (not offered 2025-26) , or 3800 (offered Fall 2025 and Spring 2026).

- ** BIBL 2000 is a prerequisite for this course
- ***BIBL 2000 and/or CMIN 2000 are the prerequisite(s) for this course

## Christian Ministries Minor (16 credit hours)

- CMIN 2000, Introduction to Christian Ministry, 3 credit hours
- 9 hours chosen from CMIN courses
- 1 hour of CMIN 2810: Ministry Exploration
- 3 upper-division hours from additional courses offered by the department
