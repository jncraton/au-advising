## Cybersecurity Major + National Security Studies Major

120 total credit hours

Cybersecurity majors add + 13 credit hours - 1 LA requirement to earn NSS BA, or

National Security Studies majors + 26 credit hours to earn Cybersecurity BA

Note: All requirements for both majors must be met, as well as requirements for the Liberal Arts Core Curriculum.
