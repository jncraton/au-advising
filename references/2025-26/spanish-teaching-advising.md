## Spanish Education Major (P-12), 4 Year Plan (91-93 credit hours) 2018-2019

## Major Requirements:

## Education Core (46 hrs)

-  EDUC 2000 Teaching as a Profession, 2 credit hours - one elementary &amp; one secondary field experience
-  EDUC 2100 Child and Adolescent Development, 3 credit hours
-  EDUC 2110 Educational Psychology: Learning in the School, 3 credit hours
-  EDUC 2460 Exploring the Multicultural Classroom, 3 credit hours
-  EDUC 2520 Technology in the Classroom, 3 credit hours
-  EDUC 2860 Practicum in Secondary Education, 1 hr secondary field experience
-  EDUC 3000 Teaching Literacy Skills in Middle and High School Content Areas, 3 credit hours
-  EDUC 3100 The Middle School in the United States, 3 credit hours secondary field experience
-  EDUC 3120 Educational Assessment for the Classroom Teacher, 3 credit hours
-  EDUC 4010 Student Teaching, 10 credit hours elementary and/or secondary field experience
-  EDUC 4710 Mgmt., Organization, and Methods in the Sec. Classroom OR EDUC 4120 Organization and Management for the Elementary Classroom, 4 credit hours elementary or secondary field experience
-  EDUC 4930 Leadership Seminar in Character Education, 2 credit hours
-  FLAN 4700 Methods in Teaching World Languages, 3 credit hours elementary and secondary field experience
-  SPED 2400 Introduction to Mild Disabilities, 3 credit hours

## Spanish Coursework (45-47 hours)

-  SPAN 2020 Intermediate Spanish II, 4 credit hours
-  SPAN 3010 Spanish Composition, 4 credit hours
-  SPAN 3020 Spanish Conversation, 4 credit hours
-  SPAN 3140 Spanish Phonetics, 3 credit hours
-  SPAN 3200 Advanced Spanish Grammar and Translation, 3 credit hours
-  SPAN 3400 Spanish Civilization and Culture, 3 credit hours
-  SPAN 3440 Spanish-American Civilization, 3 credit hours
-  SPAN 4350 or 4360, Survey of Latin American Literature I or II, 3-4 credit hours
-  SPAN 4370 or 4380, Survey of Peninsular Literature I or II, 3-4 credit hours
-  other SPAN captioned coursework until at least 35* SPAN hours are attained *At least 6 of the 35 required SPAN hours must be earned through an approved study abroad program

.

-  one 3-4 hour course in one other modern foreign language (such as FREN or GERM 1010)
-  either an additional 3-4 SPAN hours (beyond the 35) or an additional 3-4 hour course in another modern foreign language (such as FREN or GERM 1010 or 1020)
-  FLAN 3500 Study Abroad Experience, 1 credit hour (C/NC)
-  FLAN 4900 Senior Seminar in Foreign Languages, 3 credit hours

The proposed course sequence will vary based upon a student's initial language placement. On the next pages, please find sample course sequences for students placing into SPAN 2010, 2020 or 3010. Students entering at SPAN 1010 or 1020 can also complete this major and are encouraged to consult the Spanish Education advisor in the Department of Modern Foreign Languages and Cultures to plan how best to do so.

NOTE: All students must complete 120 total credits, regardless of major, to graduate.
