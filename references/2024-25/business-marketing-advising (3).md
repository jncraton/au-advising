## Marketing Major (61 credit hours)

Courses in Marketing provide essential skills for careers in advertising, brand management, market research, and digital marketing. Students learn to develop strategic marketing plans, conduct market analysis, and leverage digital platforms to engage consumers. This program also prepares students for advanced studies in marketing and related fields.
