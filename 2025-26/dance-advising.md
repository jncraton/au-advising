## Dance Performance Major (54 credit hours)

## Major Requirements:

## 17 hours in Required Courses:

- DANC 1580, Dance Improvisation I, 1 credit hour
- DANC 1590, Dance Composition I, 1 credit hour
- DANC 2000, Dance as a Profession, 1 credit hour
- DANC 3000, Dance in the Global Community, 3 credit hours
- DANC 3010, Dance History Survey, 3 credit hours
- 3 hours from DANC 1220-4220 (modern technique)
- 3 hours from DANC 1420-4420 (ballet technique)
- 2 hours from DANC 1320-4320 (jazz technique)

## 37 hours in Dance Performance Track

## Technique (17 hours)

- 3 hours from DANC 2220-4220 (modern technique)
- 3 hours from DANC 2420-4420 (ballet technique)
- 6 hours of DANC 1150, Dance Repertory, 0-1 credit hour
- DANC 2110, Musical Theatre Dance Forms, 1 credit hour
- DANC 2130, World Dance Forms, 1 credit hour
- DANC 3530, Partnering for Ballet and Modern Dance, 1 credit hour
- DANC 4910, Seminar in Audition Techniques for Dancers, 2 credit hours

## Theory (15 hours)

- DANC 2580, Dance Improvisation II, 1 credit hour
- DANC 2590, Dance Composition II, 1 credit hour
- DANC 3050, Dance Pedagogy, 3 credit hours

## or

- DANC 3060, Movement Analysis, 3 credit hours
- DANC 3510, Period Dance, 2 credit hours
- DANC 3580, Dance Improvisation III, 1 credit hour
- DANC 3590, Dance Composition III, 1 credit hour
- DANC 4500, Dance Anatomy and Kinesiology, 3 credit hours
- DANC 4590, Dance Composition IV, 1 credit hour

## Related Courses, 5 hours from:

COMM 2200, BSNS 2710, 2810, Music applied study, class study, or ensembles, THEA 2110, 2120, 2210, 3550, DANC 1150, 1650, 2000, 2220, 2320, 2420, 2650, 3220, 3320, 3420, 3520, 3650, 4220, 4420, 4650, 4660, 4800, 49002,4910

Participation in off-campus professional workshops, seminars, conferences related to field. Example ACDA, NDEO, professional development courses (Trinity Laban), Asia Pacific Dance Festival.

Prepares students for performance, Theatre, and choreography careers, graduate degrees-dance, performance &amp; choreography, history, global dance/global studies, dance ministry, dance anthropology, cultural studies, performance studies.

All Dance majors and minors require an audition for admittance to the Dance Program
