## Professional Development Major

## Major Requirements:

1. A plan of study must be approved by the Director of Professional Development before admission to the major is granted.
2. A concentration of 30 hours in one subject area must be completed and 50% must be from AU.
3. LART 4500 Critical Thinking and Professional Development (3 hours)
4. Must complete 120 hours overall (major concentration area + liberal arts requirements + LART 4500 + electives) to earn the degree (BA).
5. At least 30 hours of the required 120 hours for the degree (BA) must be completed at the 3000 and 4000 upper division level.
6. At least 60 hours of the required 120 hours for the degree (BA) must be completed at Anderson University, including the last 24 hours applicable to the degree (Under some circumstances, and with special permission, a maximum of six hours of the final 24 hours may be taken elsewhere. Contact the Office of the University Registrar for more information).
7. Completion of the Anderson University Liberal Arts Curriculum
8. Minimum GPA of 2.0 in major concentration and overall
