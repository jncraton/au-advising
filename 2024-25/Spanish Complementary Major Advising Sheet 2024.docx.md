## Spanish Complementary Major (30 credit hours)

\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

Students must complete 27 SPAN hours including the classes listed below. Note that students must complete either: 6 SPAN hours through an approved study abroad experience OR 5 SPAN hours through an approved intensive experience and 1 hour of SPAN 3240.

- SPAN 2020, Intermediate Spanish II, 4 credit hours
- SPAN 3010, Spanish Composition, 4 credit hours
- SPAN 3020, Spanish Conversation, 4 credit hours
- SPAN 3140, Spanish Phonetics, 3 credit hours
- One civilization course, typically:
- -- SPAN 3400, Spanish Civilization and Culture, 3 credit hours OR
- -- SPAN 3440, Latin American Civilization, 3 credit hours
- SPAN 4300, Literature of the Hispanic World, 3-4 credit hours
- Other SPAN-captioned coursework beyond 2010 until at least 27 SPAN hours are attained, for example:
- -- SPAN 21XX, Spanish for the Professions, 3 credit hours
- -- SPAN 3240, Strategies for Advanced Spanish Proficiency, 1-2 credit hours

## Students must also complete:

- MLAN 4900, Senior Seminar in Cultural Studies, 3 credit hours

The proposed course sequence that follows will vary based upon a student's initial language placement, primary major, and the timing of the student's Intensive Experience/Study Abroad coursework.

NOTE: All students must complete 120 total credits hours to graduate from Anderson University.

## Proposed Course Sequence:

Freshman:

SPAN 2010/2020/3010; SPAN 2020/3020

Sophomore:

SPAN 3010/3140/Civilization/Literature/Elective;

SPAN 3020/3140/Civilization/Literature/Elective

Junior:

Civilization/Literature/Elective/SPAN 3140; Civilization/Literature/Elective

Study Abroad:

Civilization/Literature/Elective, Civilization/Literature/Elective, OR SPAN 4550

Intensive Experience and SPAN 3240

Senior:

MLAN 4900, Civilization/Literature/Elective/SPAN 3140
