## Music Business: (53 credit hours)

\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

The music business major prepares students for a career in one of the following EARS tracks : E ntrepreneurship, A udio Forensics, R ecord production, and S ongwriting.  This major requires completion of courses in music, music performance, music business, and business. Students must complete an internship in music business, tailoring an in-depth experience in their chosen music industry track.

## Core Music Requirements: (23 credit hours)

- MUSC 1010, Theory I, 3 credit hours
- MUSC 1020, Theory II, 3 credit hours
- MUSC 1030, Aural Comprehension I, 1 credit hour
- MUSC 1040, Aural Comprehension II, 1 credit hour
- MUSC 2210 Music, the Arts, and Culture, 3 credit hours
- MUPF 1050, Keyboard Class I, 2 credit hours
- MUPF 1060, Keyboard Class II, 2 credit hours
- MUPF Applied Private Study (4 credit hours), 1-3 credit hours (may be repeated)
- MUPF Ensemble participation (4 credit hours, 0-1 credit hours (may be repeated)

## Core Music Business Requirements: (18 credit hours)

- MUBS 2010 Introduction to the Music Business, 3 credit hours
- MUBS 2020 Introduction to Music Publishing, 3 credit hours
- MUBS 3310/BSNS 3320 Legal Aspects of the Music Business, 2 credit hours
- MUBS 3350/BSNS 3330 Music Marketing, 3 credit hours
- MUBS 4800 Internship in Music Business, 1-3 credit hours
- MUBS 4900 Recording Label Seminar, 4 credit hours

## Music Business electives: (12 credit hours)

- BSNS 4400 Social Media, 3 credit hours
- CRIM 2520 Introduction to Criminal Justice, 3 credit hours
- MUBS 2070 Songwriting, 2 credit hours
- MUBS 3100 Artist Development, 2 credit hours
- MUBS 3210 Recording Techniques, 3 credit hours
- MUBS 3220 Advanced Recording Techniques, 2 credit hours
- MUBS 3370 Record Production, 2 credit hours
- MUBS 3380 Concert Booking and Promotion, 2 credit hours
- MUBS 3450 Live Sound Production, 2 credit hours
- MUBS 3470 DIY for Musicians, 2 credit hours
- MUBS 3550 Audio Enhancement and Authentication, 3 credit hours
- MUBS 4500 Songwriter Showcase, 1 credit hour
- MUSC 4950 Senior Project, 3 credit hours
- MUPF 2905, 4905 Songwriting Applied Lessons, 6 credit hours An English Minor or Writing Minor is recommended for students in the Songwriting Track

NOTE: All students must complete 120 total credit hours to graduate from Anderson University.
