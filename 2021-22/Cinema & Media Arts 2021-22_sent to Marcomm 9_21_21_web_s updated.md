## Cinema &amp; Media Arts Major (52 credit hours)

\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

The Cinema &amp; Media Arts major provides education and experience for students in all areas of audio, video, and cinema production. From as early as their freshman year, students have the opportunity to gain hands-on professional experience in all production classes and through Black Bird Media Lab.

## Major Requirements:

- COMM 2000, Media and Society, 3 credit hours
- COMM 2010, The Communication Professional, 1 credit hour
- COMM 2020, Audio Concepts &amp; Production, 3 credit hours
- COMM 2060, Video/Cinema Concepts, 3 credit hours
- COMM 21 60, The Art of Storytelling, 3 credit hours
- COMM 2200, Visual Communication, 3 credit hours
- COMM 2320, Television Studio Production, 4 credit hours
- COMM 2420, Field, Soundstage, and Postproduction, 4 credit hours
- COMM 3120, The Business of Radio/TV/Film, 3 credit hours
- COMM 3200, Communication Ethics, 3 credit hours
- COMM 3220, Screenwriting, 3 credit hours
- COMM 3420, Cinema Studies, 3 credit hours
- COMM 4000, Communication Law, 3 credit hours
- COMM 4800, Internship, 1 -4 credit hours

## 4 credit hours from:

- COMM 2860, Cinema and Media Arts Practicum, 1 credit hour

## Remaining hours from these electives:

- COMM 3050, Global Communication, 3 credit hours
- COMM 3160, Motion Graphics, 3 credit hours
- COMM 3 260, Television Scriptwriting, 3 credit hours
- COMM 4120, Advanced Video Production, 3 credit hours
- COMM 4900, Seminar in Communication, 3 credit hours (offered periodically)
- THEA 2210, Stagecraft 3 credit hours
- THEA 2110, Acting I, 3 credit hours
- ENGL 3140, Writing and Digital Media, 3 credit hours

NOTE: All students must complete 120 total credit hours to graduate from Anderson University.
