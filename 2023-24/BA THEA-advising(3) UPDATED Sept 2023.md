## Theatre Major (42 credit hours)

## Major Requirements:

## Required Courses (34 credit hours):

- THEA 2110, Acting I, 3 credit hours
- THEA 2210, Stagecraft, 3 credit hours
- THEA 2220, Theatre Genres and Analysis, 2 credit hours
- THEA 2410, Voice and Movement I, 2 credit hours
- THEA 3010, History of Theatre I, 3 credit hours
- THEA 3020, History of Theatre II, 3 credit hours
- THEA 3400, Directing, 3 credit hours
- THEA 3500, Scene Design, 3 credit hours
- THEA 3550, Lighting Design, 3 credit hours
- THEA 4800, Senior Project, 1 credit hour
- THEA 2890, Acting/Production Practicum (eight registrations), 1 credit hour each of 8 semesters

## Elective Courses - 8 elective credit hours from:

- THEA 2120, Acting II, 3 credit hours
- THEA 2420, Voice &amp; Movement II, 2 credit hours
- THEA 3110, Acting III, 3 credit hours
- THEA 3120, Acting IV, 3 credit hours
- MUPF 2700, Voice, (up to 2 credit hours), 1 hour each of 2 semesters
- MUPF 4910, Audition Techniques, 2 credit hours
- MUPF 1070-1390, Music Ensemble (up to 2 credit hours), 1 credit hour each of 2 semesters
- DANC XXXX, Dance Techniques courses (up to 2 credit hours), 1 hour each of 2 semesters
