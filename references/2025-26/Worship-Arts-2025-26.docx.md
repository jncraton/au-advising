## Worship Arts Major (53 credit hours)

## Major Requirements:

- MUSC 1010, Theory I, 3 credit hours
- MUSC 1030, Aural Comprehension I, 1 credit hour
- MUSC 3150, Art, Faith, and Culture (WI), 3 credit hours
- MUSC 3160, Sacred Music: Past and Current Uses, 3 credit hours
- MUSC 3800, Internship, 3 credit hours
- MUED 2470, Introduction to Conducting, 2 credit hours
- MUPF 1050, Keyboard Class I (or private piano lesson), 2 credit hours
- MUPF 1410, Guitar Class (or private guitar lesson), 1 credit hour
- MUPF XXXX, Private Music Lessons (voice or piano), 4 credit hours
- MUPF XXXX, Ensembles, 4 credit hours
- MUBS 2020, Introduction to Music Publishing, 3 credit hours
- MUBS 3450, Live Sound Production, 2 credit hours
- THEA 2350, Introduction to Acting, 2 credit hours
- DANC 1580, Dance Improvisation I, 1 credit hour
- DANC 1590, Dance Composition I, 1 credit hour
- COMM 2140,*Producing Multimedia Content, 3 credit hours
- COMM 2200, Visual Communication, 3 credit hours

*Prerequisites for COMM 2140 are waived for students in the Worship Arts Major 12 credit hours from:

- CMIN 2000, Introduction to Christian Ministry, 3 credit hours
- CMIN 2270, Faith and Worship, 3 credit hours
- CMIN 3050, Homiletics, 3 credit hours
- RLGN 3040, Introduction to Christian Theology, 3 credit hours
- RLGN/HIST 3060, History of Christianity, 3 credit hours
- RLGN/HIST 3420, American Religious History, 3 credit hours

NOTE: All students must complete 120 total credit hours to graduate from Anderson University.
