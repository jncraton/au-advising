The International Relations major is an innovative opportunity for AU students to tailor their studies to fit our rapidly changing world. This major was designed for students desiring to be better prepared to work in the global environment, whether they actually work beyond the United States or within its borders.

* SOCI 4810 is required only for the students with the Global Health concentration; the course may be taken during the summer.
