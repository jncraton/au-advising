## Applied Study

-  22 credit hours of private study in the primary performance area, including a half recital in junior year and a full recital in senior year
-  7 credit hours of major ensembles
-  2 credit hours of chamber ensembles

## Proposed course sequence:

Freshman:

MUSC 1010, 1030, MUPF 1050, Private Instruction, Ensemble, MUPF 1520* (pianists only);

MUSC 1020, 1040, MUPF 1060, Private Instruction, Ensemble, MUPF 1530* (pianists only)

Sophomore:

MUSC 2010, 2030, 2110, MUPF 1710, MUED 2470, Private Instruction, Ensemble; MUSC 2020, 2040, 3220, MUPF 2030, Private Instruction, Ensemble

Junior:

MUBS 2050, MUED 3480, 3370*(pianists only), MUED 3130* (except pianists), Private Instruction, Ensemble; MUSC 3040, 3380*(pianists only), MUED 3330*(except pianists), Private Instruction, Ensemble, Recital

Senior:

MUBS 3360, MUSC 3110, MUPF 2060, Private Instruction, Ensemble, Chamber Ensemble; MUSC 3120, Private Instruction, Chamber Ensemble, Recital

Due to the variability of rehearsal schedules, please consult your academic adviser for scheduling Liberal Arts classes.
