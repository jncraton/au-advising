## TeachScience Complementary Major (30 credit hours)

2018-2019

TeachScience Complimentary Majors: must accompany a biology, chemistry, or physics major.

## Major Requirements:

- BIOL/CHEM/PHYS 2700, Science Teaching Methods I, 3 credit hours
- EDUC 2000, Teaching as a Profession, 2 credit hours
- EDUC 3000, Teaching Literacy Skills in Middle and High School content Areas, 3 credit hours
- EDUC 3120, Educational Assessment for the Classroom Teacher, 3 credit hours
- EDUC 4010, Student Teaching, 10-24 credit hours
- EDUC 4710, Management, Organization, and Methods in the Secondary Classroom, 4 credit hours
- EDUC 4930, Leadership Seminar in Character Education, 2 credit hours
- SPED 2400, Introduction to Mild Disabilities, 3 credit hours

## Proposed course sequence:

Freshman:

EDUC 2000

Sophomore:

SPED 2400

Junior:

EDUC 3000, EDUC 2460

Senior:

EDUC 3120, 4710; EDUC 4010, 4930

- EDUC 3120, Educational Assessment for the Classroom Teacher, is a Writing Intensive course in the Liberal Arts Program.
- EDUC 4710, Management, Organization, and Methods in the Secondary Classroom, is a Writing Intensive course in the Liberal Arts Program.

Students wanting a teaching license in the state of Indiana are strongly encouraged to take: ENGL 1400, EDUC 2110, HIST 2110 or 2120, and EDUC 2460.
