## Multimedia Journalism Complementary Major (30 credit hours)\_\_

\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

In this complementary major, students are prepared for careers in reporting, writing and editing in both the print and electronic media environments. Practical experiences include producing news stories in classes, writing for the professional press, interning at a variety of news organizations, and working on newscast and online content for the student-operated news organization, the Andersonian.

## Major Requirements:

- COMM 2060   Video/Cinema Concepts (3 hours)
- COMM 2130   Writing for the Media (3 hours)
- COMM 2200   Visual Communication (3 hours)
- COMM 2850   Andersonian Staff (1 hour, PRQ 2130)
- COMM 2850   Andersonian Staff (1 hour, PRQ 2130)
- COMM 2850   Andersonian Staff (1 hour, PRQ 2130)
- COMM 2860   Cinema and Media Arts Practicum (1 hour, PRQ 2060)
- COMM 3130   Advanced Newswriting and Reporting (3 hours, PRQ 2130)
- COMM 3230   Advanced Feature and Digital Writing (3 hours, PRQ 2130)
- COMM 4800   Internship (2 hours)
- Choose one:
- COMM 3200   Communication Ethics (3 hours, PRQ 2000)
- COMM 4000   Communication Law (3 hours, PRQ 2000)
- Choose one:
- COMM 2140   Producing Multimedia Content (3 hours)
- COMM 3370   Communicating through Social Media (3 hours)

NOTE: All students must complete 120 total credit hours to graduate from Anderson University.
