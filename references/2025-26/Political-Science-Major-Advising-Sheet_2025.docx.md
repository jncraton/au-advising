## Political Science Major (36 credit hours)

The Political Science major is designed to transform students with an interest in or concern for politics into graduates who can understand, evaluate, manage, and shape political events and governmental actions in a manner consistent with a Christian faith perspective. The program involves the systematic study of how political institutions influence the behavior of citizens, leaders, and international actors to achieve public policies that promote justice both nationally and globally. Our program will help you prepare for successful careers in government, politics, law, non-profits, teaching, journalism, business, and more.

## Major Requirements:

Foundational Courses (16 credit hours):

- POSC 2020, Introduction to World Politics, 3 credit hours
- POSC 2100, American National Government, 3 credit hours
- POSC 2200, Public Policy, 3 credit hours
- POSC 2400, Social Science Research Methods, 3 credit hours
- MATH 2120, Introductory Statistics with Applications, 3 credit hours

American Politics courses (6 credit hours, two courses from):

- POSC 3140, Elections, Public Opinion, and Democracy, 3 credit hours
- POSC 3211, The Congress, 3 credit hours (fulfills Writing Intensive and Speaking Intensive)
- POSC 3212, The Presidency, 3 credit hours (fulfills Speaking Intensive)

International Relations and Comparative Politics courses (6 credit hours, two courses from):

- POSC 3300, American Foreign Policy, 3 credit hours
- POSC 3400, Comparative Politics, 3 credit hours
- POSC 3510, International Political Economy, 3 credit hours

Senior Capstone course (1 credit hour):

- POSC 4930, Senior Seminar, 1 credit hour

Electives (8 credit hours):

- Remaining hours are chosen from additional POSC courses; at least six (6) hours from upper-division (3000-level or above), not previously taken.

N o more than 5 credit hours from POSC 2810, 4800, 4810 and 4820 may be applied toward the major.

NOTE: All students must complete 120 total credit hours to graduate from Anderson University.
