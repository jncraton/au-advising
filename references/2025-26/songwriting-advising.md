## Songwriting Major (44 credit hours)

## Major Requirements:

## Music (Songwriting Core) 12 credit hours:

- MUSC 1010, Music Theory I, 3 credit hours
- MUSC 1030, Aural Comprehension I, 1 credit hour
- MUSC 2110, Introduction to Music Literature, 2 credit hours
- MUPF 1050, Keyboard I (may be waived by exam), 2 credit hours
- MUPF 1700-4890, Private Lessons, 2 hours
- MUPF 2900, Composition (Songwriting), 2 credit hours

## Music Business 9 credit hours:

- MUBS 2010, Introduction to Music Business, 3 credit hours
- MUBS 2020, Introduction to Music Publishing, 3 credit hours
- MUBS 2070, Songwriting, 2 credit hours
- MUBS 4500, Songwriting Showcase, 1 credit hour
- BSNS 3330, Music Marketing, 3 credit hours

## English 14 credit hours:

- ENGL 2400, Introduction to Literature, 4 credit hours
- ENGL 2500, Appreciation of the Writing Craft, 2 credit hours
- ENGL 2510, Creative Writing Workshop, 1 credit hour
- ENGL 3120, Creative Writing:  Poetry, 3 credit hours

## Literature (choose one):

- ENGL 3320, Shakespeare, 4 credit hours
- ENGL 3340, The Renaissance in England, 4 credit hours
- ENGL 3390, British Literature (19 th Century), 4 credit hours
- ENGL 4550, American Poetry, 4 credit hours

## Music Business Elective 6 credit hours:

- MUBS 3100, Artist Development, 2 credit hours
- MUBS 3210, Recording Techniques I, 3 credit hours
- MUBS 3220, Recording Techniques II, 2 credit hours
- MUBS 3310, Legal Aspects of the Music Industry, 2 credit hours
- MUBS 3360, Beyond Talent:  Entrepreneurship, 2 credit hours
- MUBS 4800, Internship in Music Business, 1-3 credit hours
- MUBS 4900, Record Label Seminar, 1-4 credit hours

## Proposed Course Sequence:

Freshman:

MUSC 1010, MUSC 1030, MUPF 1050, Private Music Lesson; MUBS 2070, Private Music Lesson

Sophomore:  MUSC 2110, MUPF 2900, MUBS 2010; MUBS 2020, MUPF 2900, MUBS 2020

Junior:

BSNS 3330, Music Business Elective, ENGL 2400; Music Business Elective, ENGL 2500, ENGL 3120

Senior:

Music Business Elective, ENGL 2510; MUBS 4500, English Literature Elective
