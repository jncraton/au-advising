## Language Arts Teaching Major (44 credit hours)

The language arts teaching major is designed to prepare the student for teaching in grades 6-12. It includes a 38-hour core of courses with English and speech captions, thus qualifying the student to teach English and speech courses in grades 6-12, and 6 additional hours of advanced writing.

## Required courses (38 credit hours):

- ENGL 2400, Introduction to Literature, 3 credit hours
- ENGL 2220, Global Literature, 3 credit hours
- ENGL 3000, English Grammar and Style, 3 credit hours
- ENGL 3050, History of the English Language, 3 credit hours
- ENGL 3320, Shakespeare, 3 credit hours
- ENGL 3540, Studies in Early British Literature, 3 credit hours
- ENGL 3560, Studies in Later British Literature, 3 credit hours
- ENGL 3570, Studies in American Literature, 3 credit hours
- ENGL 3580, American Ethnic Literature, 3 credit hours
- ENGL 3590, Teaching Young Adult Literature, 3 credit hours
- ENGL 4700, Methods of Teaching English* (co-requisite), 3 credit hours
- ENGL 3140, Writing and Digital Media, 3 credit hours
- ENGL 3880, Tutor Practicum, 1 credit hour
- COMM 4750, Strategies for Teaching Speech* (co-requisite), 1 credit hour

Note: ENGL 4700 and COMM 4750 should be taken prior to your professional semester.

Note also that ENGL 4700 is offered in the fall only.

## One course from each of the following pairs (6 credit hours):

- ENGL 3110, Creative Writing Fiction OR ENGL 3120, Creative Writing Poetry, 3 credit hours
- ENGL 3180 Advanced Composition: Non-fiction OR ENGL 3190 Advanced Composition:  Argument, 3 credit hours

## Liberal Arts Requirements:

- Majors are required to take HIST 2110 or 2120 for the Civic Ways of Knowing liberal arts requirement.
- Majors are required to take ENGL 3590 for the Aesthetic Ways of Knowing liberal arts requirements.
- Writing Intensive courses: EDUC 4710, ENGL 3110/3120, ENGL 3180/3190, ENGL 3580, ENGL 4700
- ENGL 3580 satisfies the Civil Discourse and Critical Reasoning requirement
- Speaking Intensive courses: ENGL 2220
- ENGL 3050 is both writing intensive AND speaking intensive
