## *History Major (36 credit hours)

\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

The history major stresses the historical and scientific approach to give the student an understanding of the origin and development of civilization, past and present. Courses equip students with the necessary tools to: evaluate present trends, to prepare for intelligent and active citizenship, for teaching and related professions, and for graduate study.

## Major Requirements (18 credit hours):

## One from:

- HIST 2030 Western Civilization I, 3 credit hours (fulfills Civic Ways of Knowing)
- HIST 2040 Western Civilization II, 3 credit hours (fulfills Civic Ways of Knowing)

## One from:

- HIST 2110 American Civilization I, 3 credit hours (fulfills Civic Ways of Knowing)
- HIST 2120 American Civilization II, 3 credit hours (fulfills Civic Ways of Knowing)

## Required:

- HIST 2000 World Civilization, 3 credit hours (fulfills Civic Ways of Knowing)
- HIST 2300 Historical Inquiry, 3 credit hours (fulfills: Speaking Intensive; Civil Discourse and Critical Reasoning)
- HIST 2350 Foundation of Public History, 3 credit hours (fulfills Speaking Intensive)
- HIST 4800 History Internship, 2 credit hours (fulfills Experiential Learning)
- HIST 4930 Senior Seminar, 1 credit hour (fulfills Speaking Intensive)
