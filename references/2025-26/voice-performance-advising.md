## Voice Performance Major (80 credit hours)

## Major Requirements:

## 31 credit hours from:

- MUSC 1010, Theory I, 3 credit hours
- MUSC 1020, Theory II, 3 credit hours
- MUSC 1030, Aural Comprehension I, 1 credit hour
- MUSC 1040, Aural Comprehension II, 1 credit hour
- MUSC 2010, Theory III, 3 credit hours
- MUSC 2020, Theory IV, 3 credit hours
- MUSC 2030, Aural Comprehension III, 1 credit hour
- MUSC 2040, Aural Comprehension IV, 1 credit hour
- MUSC 2110, Introduction to Music Literature, 2 credit hours
- MUSC 3120, History of Music: Baroque/Classic, 3 credit hours
- MUSC 3130, History of Music: 19 th and 20 th Centuries, 3 credit hours
- MUSC 3220, World Music, 3 credit hours
- MUBS 3360, Beyond Talent: Entrepreneurship for Musicians, 2 credit hours
- MUED 2470, Introduction to Conducting, 2 credit hours

## 4 credit hours from:

- MUPF 1050, Keyboard Class I, 2 credit hours
- MUPF 1060, Keyboard Class II, 2 credit hours
- MUPF 1710, Piano, 1 credit hour
- MUPF 2710, Piano, 1 credit hour
- MUPF 2030, Advanced Small-Group Piano I, 1 credit hour
- MUPF 2040, Advanced Small-Group Piano II, 1 credit hour

## 2 credit hours from:

- MUPF 4910, Seminar in Audition Techniques, 2 credit hours

## Pedagogy &amp; Literature

- MUED 2510, Singer's Diction I, 2 credit hours
- MUED 2520, Singer's Diction II, 2 credit hours
- MUED 3350, Voice Pedagogy, 2 credit hours
- MUSC 3390, Song Literature, 3 credit hours

## Applied Study

18 credit hours from applied study, including a half recital in junior year and a full recital in senior year

## Related Skills

- MUTR 2140, Voice &amp; Movement I, 2 credit hours
- THEA 2110, Acting I, 3 credit hours
- THEA 2120, Acting II, 3 credit hours
- THEA 2210, Stagecraft, 3 credit hours

## 4 credit hours from:

- MUPF 1150, Opera Production, 0-1 credit hours
- MUPF 1160, Musical Theatre Production, 0-1 credit hours
- MUPF 1170, Lyric Theatre Workshop, 0-1 credit hours
