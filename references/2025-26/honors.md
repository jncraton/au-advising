Honors Program (15-19 credit hours total)

2025-2026

## Program Requirements:

1st Semester Freshman Year:

- HNRS 2110 Honors Literature and Writing, 3 credit hours (Fall)

Choose four of the following six courses to be completed between the spring semester of your freshman year and the spring semester of your junior year:

- HNRS 2125 The Scholar as Servant Leader, 2 hours (Spring)
- HNRS 2300 Honors History, 3 hours (Spring)
- HNRS 2210 Scientific Discoveries and Paradigm Shifts, 4 hours (Fall)
- HNRS 3221 The Scholar as Citizen of the World: Traditionalism and Globalization, 3 hours (Spring)
- HNRS 3100 Art and the Human Response, 3 hours (Fall)
- HNRS 3325 Christ and Culture, 3 hours (Spring)

Senior Year (Can be completed during the junior year if a student plans to graduate in 3 years):

- HNRS 4910 Honors Senior Seminar (Fall/Fall + Spring)

Can be completed during any semester:

- HNRS 1000 Experiential Learning Through Servant Leadership, 1 or 0 Credits (Fall/Spring)
