## Social Studies Teaching Major (51 SSE credit hours + 43 education credit hours)

The Social Studies Teaching major is designed for students wanting to teach social studies at the middle-school or high-school level. Students must complete specific courses in three teaching fields and in professional education. The required teaching fields are historical perspectives; government and citizenship; and a third area chosen from sociology, economics, psychology, or special education. In addition to these requirements, students pursuing a teaching major must also meet additional requirements listed in the School of Education section of the undergraduate catalog.

- HIST 4700, Methods of Teaching Social Studies, 3 credit hours
- ECON 2010, PSYC 2000, SOCI 2010, 9 hours total
