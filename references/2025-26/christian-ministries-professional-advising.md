## Christian Ministries Major (43 credit hours; Suggested Four Year Plan )

## Major Requirements

## Departmental Core (21 hours):

- BIBL 2000, Introduction to the Bible, 3 credit hours (offered each semester)
- BIBL 2050, Methods in Biblical Exegesis, 3 credit hours (offered semester two)**
- RLGN 2000, Introduction to Religion, 3 credit hours (offered semester one)
- RLGN 2060, History of Christianity, 3 credit hours (offered semester one)
- RLGN 2130, Introduction to Christian Ethics, 3 credit hours (offered semester one)**
- RLGN 2150, Hermeneutics: The Practice of Interpreting, 3 credit hours (offered semester one)**
- RLGN 3040, Introduction to Christian Theology, 3 credit hours (offered semester two)**
- **BIBL 2000 is a prerequisite for this course

## Ministry Core (19 hours):

- CMIN 2000, Introduction to Christian Ministry, 3 credit hours (offered each semester)
- CMIN 3050, Homiletics, 3 credit hours (offered semester one)***
- CMIN 3080, Pastoral Care and Counseling, 3 credit hours (offered semester two)***
- CMIN 3910, Ministry Seminar, 3 credit hours (offered both semesters)***
- CMIN 4250, Theology in a Ministry Context, 3 credit hours (offered semester one)***
- CMIN 4810, Ministry Internship, 1-4 credit hours (offered both semesters)***
- ***BIBL 2000 and/or CMIN 2000 is/are the prerequisite(s) for this course

Majors must take 3 additional credit hours from courses offered by the department.

The Christian Ministries major is designed to prepare students to serve as Christian leaders and servants in the world.  It offers undergraduate study of ministry which may lead to:

1. Lay ministry while pursuing another profession.
2. A position of leadership in ministry in a local congregation, a church-related organization, or a related field.
3. Graduate study in ministry.  This major is intended to be a first step in a plan of lifelong learning for ministry.
