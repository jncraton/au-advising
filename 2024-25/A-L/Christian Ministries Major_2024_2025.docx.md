## Christian Ministries Major (46 credit hours)

2024-2025\_\_\_\_\_\_

The Christian Ministries major is designed to prepare students to serve as Christian leaders and servants in the world. It offers undergraduate study of ministry which may lead to:

1. Lay ministry while pursuing another profession.
2. A position of leadership in ministry in a local congregation, a church-related organization, or a related field.
3. Graduate study in ministry. This major is intended to be a first step in a plan of lifelong learning for ministry.

## Major Requirements:

## Departmental Core (18 hours):

- BIBL 2000, Introduction to the Bible, 3 credit hours (offered semesters 1 and 2 in 2024-2025)
- BIBL 2050, Methods in Biblical Exegesis, 3 credit hours** (not on 2024-2025 schedule)
- RLGN 2000, Introduction to Religion, 3 credit hours (offered semester 2 in 2024-2025)
- RLGN 2130, Introduction to Christian Ethics, 3 credit hours** (offered semester 2 in 2024-2025)
- RLGN 3040, Introduction to Christian Theology, 3 credit hours** (not on 2024-2025 schedule)
- RLGN 3060, History of Christianity, 3 credit hours (not on 2024-2025 schedule)

## Ministry Core (16 hours):

- CMIN 2810, Ministry Exploration, 1 credit hour (offered semesters 1 and 2 in 2024-2025)
- CMIN 3050, Homiletics, 3 credit hours*** (offered semesters 1 and 2 in 2024-2025)
- CMIN 3080, Pastoral Care and Counseling, 3 credit hours *** (not on 2024-2025 schedule)
- CMIN 3910, Ministry Seminar, 3 hours*** (not on 2024-2025 schedule)
- CMIN 4250, Theology in a Ministry Context, 3 credit hours*** (offered semester 2 in 2024-2025)
- ·Three hours from internship courses: CMIN 4810 (offered semesters 1 and 2 in 2024-2025) or MUSC 3800 (offered semester 2 in 2024-2025)
- ·Nine (9) additional credit hours from courses offered by the department BIBL, CMIN, PHIL, or RLGN captions) or MUSC 3150, 3160, or 3800
- ** BIBL 2000 is a prerequisite for this course
- ***BIBL 2000 and/or CMIN 2000 are the prerequisite(s) for this course

## Christian Ministries Minor (16 credit hours)

- CMIN 2000, Introduction to Christian Ministry, 3 credit hours
- 9 hours chosen from CMIN courses
- 1 hour of CMIN 2810: Ministry Exploration
