## ANDERSON UNIVERSITY

## Elementary Education Major (83 credit hours)

The Elementary Education Teaching major is designed to prepare students for teaching in an elementary classroom and meet the licensure requirements for the state of Indiana.

## Major Requirements:

## Department ofTeacher Education Requirements (66 credits):

- EDUC 2000, Teaching as a Profession, 2 credit hours
- EDUC 2030, Learning and Teaching Mathematics in the Elementary Classroom, 3 credit hours
- EDUC 2100, Child and Adolescent Development, 3 credit hours
- EDUC 2110, Educational Psychology, 3 credit hours
- EDUC 2170, Teaching I: Curriculum and Instruction for Early Childhood, 4 credit hours
- EDUC 2200, Foundations in Reading Instruction, 3 credit hours
- EDUC 2460, Teaching in a Diverse Society, 3 credit hours
- EDUC 2520, Technology in the Classroom, 3 credit hours
- EDUC 2730, Field Experiences in Reading Processes, 1 credit hour

## Elementary Education Cognate (17 credit hours):

- EDUC 4850, Reading Clinic, 4 credit hours
- EDUC 4910, Reading Research Seminar, 1 credit hour
- HIST 2000, History of World Civilization, 3 credit hours
- MATH 1110, Elementary Math Geometry, 3 credit hours
- PETE 3710, Instructional Strategies (K-5), 3 credit hours
- PHYS 1030, Teaching STEM in the Elementary Classroom, 3 credit hours

Embedded in the elementary education major is a 12-credit reading concentration that can serve as the required concentration for state licensing.
