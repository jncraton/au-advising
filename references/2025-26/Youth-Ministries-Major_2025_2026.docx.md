## Youth Ministries Major (49 credit hours)

2025-2026

Youth Ministries is a stand-alone major that prepares students for ministry to youth, including church-based youth and children's ministries, youth camps, urban ministries, or other settings. The major is strongly rooted in Bible and ministry courses, which prepares students not only for immediate ministry work, but also for further graduate theological education.

The Youth Ministries Major is also available as a 4+1 option where students can complete both the Bachelor's degree in Youth Ministry as well as an MDIV in 5 years. Please contact the department for more information on the 4+1, or look at the Christian Ministries 4+1 Advising sheet as an example.

## Major Requirements:

## Departmental Core (18 hours):

- BIBL 2000, Introduction to the Bible, 3 credit hours (offered Fall 2025 and Spring 2026)
- BIBL 2050, Methods in Biblical Exegesis, 3 credit hours** (offered Fall 2025)
- RLGN 2000, Introduction to Religion, 3 credit hours (not offered 2025-26)
- RLGN 2130, Introduction to Christian Ethics, 3 credit hours**  (offered 2026-27)
- RLGN 3040, Introduction to Christian Theology, 3 credit hours**  (offered Spring 2026)
- RLGN 3060, History of Christianity, 3 credit hours  (offered Fall 2025)

## Ministry Core (6 hours):

- CMIN 2000, Introduction to Church Ministry, 3 credit hours  (offered Fall 2025)
- CMIN 4250, Theology in a Ministry Context, 3 credit hours***  (offered Spring 2026)

## Additional Ministry Core courses (6 hours):

- CMIN 3050, Homiletics, 3 credit hours*** (offered online Spring 2026)
- CMIN 3080, Pastoral Care and Counseling, 3 credit hours***  (offered Fall 2025)
- CMIN 3910, Ministry Seminar***  (offered Fall 2025)

## Youth Ministry courses (6 hours):

- CMIN 2260, Educational Ministry with Youth, 3 credit hours (not offered 2025-26)
- CMIN 3260, Leading, Teaching, and Discipling Youth, 3 credit hours (not offered 2025-26)

## Internship/experiential courses (4 hours):

- CMIN 2810 Ministry Exploration + CMIN 3340, The Community of Faith in an Urban Culture, 3 credit hours (instructor's consent required - offered most terms, summer session includes travel to Atlanta),
- CMIN 4860, Practicum in Ministry, 1-4 credit hours*** (offered most terms), AND/OR
- CMIN 2810 Ministry Exploration + MUSC 3800, Internship in Church Music, 3 credit hours (offered Fall 2025 and Spring 2026)

9 additional hours from any courses offered by the department [BIBL, CMIN, PACT (all courses are 2 credit hours****), PHIL, or RLGN captions] or MUSC 3150 (not offered 2025-26) , 3160 (not offered 2025-26) , or 3800 (offered Fall 2025 and Spring 2026).

** BIBL 2000 is a prerequisite for this course

***BIBL 2000 and/or CMIN 2000 are the prerequisite(s) for this course

****A two-hour PACT course could be paired with a four-hour course in a Biblical language or a student can pick up an extra hour of practicum.
