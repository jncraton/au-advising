## Major Requirements:

1. A plan of study must be approved by the General Studies academic advisor
2. A concentration area of 15-18 hours must be completed
3. Must complete at least 30 hours of the Liberal Arts core curriculum requirements from the following categories: ENGL 1100 or 1110, ENGL 1120, COMM 1000, Quantitative Reasoning, BIBL 2000, Personal Wellness, Scientific Ways of Knowing (WoK), Civic WoK, Aesthetic WoK and Social and Behavioral WoK
4. At least 30 hours must be completed at Anderson University
5. Students must complete 60 hours overall and also meet other graduation requirements as listed in the catalog
6. This curriculum assumes proficiency in writing at the ENGL 1100 or 1110 level and in mathematics at the MATH 1000 level.
