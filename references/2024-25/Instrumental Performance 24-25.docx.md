## Applied Study

- 22 credit hours of private study in the primary performance area, including a half recital in junior year and a full recital in senior year
- 7 credit hours of major ensembles
- 2 credit hours of chamber ensembles

Due to the variability of rehearsal schedules, please consult your academic adviser for scheduling Liberal Arts classes.

NOTE: All students must complete 120 total credit hours to graduate from Anderson University.

## Proposed course sequence:

Freshman:

MUSC 1010, 1030, MUPF 1050, Private Lesson, Ensemble; MUSC 1020, 1040, MUPF 1060, Private Lesson, Ensemble

Sophomore:

MUSC 2010, 2030, 2110, MUPF 1710, MUED 2470, Private Lesson, Ensemble;

MUSC 2020, 2040, 2330, MUPF 2030, Private Lesson, Ensemble

Junior:

MUBS 2050, MUED 3480, MUED 3370/MUSC 3650, MUSC 3030/3100/4900/MUBS 2050, Private Lesson, Ensemble;

MUSC 3040, MUSC 3380/MUED 3330, MUSC 3030/3100/4900/MUBS 2050, Private Lesson, Ensemble, Recital, MUSC 3040

Senior:

MUBS 3360, MUSC 3110, MUPF 2060, Private Lesson, Ensemble, Chamber Ensemble; MUSC 3120, Private Lesson, Chamber Ensemble, Recital
