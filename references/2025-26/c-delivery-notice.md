## Affordable Care Act (ACA) Reporting

## You Can Request Your Form 1095-C

## Important information regarding health insurance coverage Tax Documents:

## No automatic distribution:

- Under ACA rules, you will not automatically receive a copy of your Form 1095-C detailing your health insurance coverage.

## What is Form 1095-C?

- Form 1095-C provides details about the health coverage offered to you by your employer.  Form 1095-C is not attached to your tax return filing but should be kept with your tax records.

## Digitally Available

- The Form 1095-Cs will be available via the Anderson University Paycom Employee Self Service Portal.  You can access the portal via this link: Paycom Online

## Request a copy:
