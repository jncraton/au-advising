## Dance Complementary Major (33 credit hours)

Allows students to pair their passion for dance with their interest in a separate, primary major area without overwhelming their semester load. The Dance Complementary major pairs with any other major on campus. The careers of students who choose this complementary major vary greatly based upon their primary area of study.

## Major Requirements:

33 credit hours in Required Courses

- DANC 1580, Dance Improvisation I, 1 credit hour
- DANC 1590, Dance Composition I, 2 credit hours
- DANC 3000 Global Dance Forms or DANC 3010 or 3020 Dance History I or II, 3 credit hours
- DANC 3050 Pedagogy or DANC 3060 Movement Analysis, 3 credit hours
- 3 hours of DANC 1150 Dance Repertory
- 3 hours of DANC 1160 Dance Production
- 2 hours from DANC 1220-4220 (modern technique)
- 2 hours from DANC 1420-4420 (ballet technique)
- 2 hours from DANC 1320-4320 (jazz technique)
- 12 hours of approved related courses

NOTE: All students must complete 120 total credit hours to graduate from Anderson University.

Allows students to complete a minor in Dance while pursuing other interests in the university.

## Minor Requirements:

18 credit hours in Required Courses

- 1 hour of DANC 1150, Dance Repertory, 1 credit hour
- 1 hour of DANC 1160, Dance Production, 1 credit hour
- 2 hours from DANC 1220-4220, Modern Technique, 1 credit hour
- 2 hours from DANC 1420-4420, Ballet Technique, 1 credit hour
- 1 hour from DANC 1320-4320, Jazz Technique, 1 credit hour
- DANC 1580, Dance Improvisation I, 1 credit hour
- DANC 1590, Dance Composition I, 2 credit hours
- DANC 3000, Global Dance Forms, 3 credit hours
- DANC 3020, Dance History II, 3 credit hours
- DANC 3510, Period Dance, 2 credit hours
