## Musical Theatre Major-BA (65 credit hours)

## Major Requirements:

## 37 credit hours:

- MUSC 1010, Theory I, 3 credit hours
- MUSC 1020, Theory II, 3 credit hours
- MUSC 1030, Aural Comprehension I, 1 credit hour
- MUSC 1040, Aural Comprehension II, 1 credit hour
- MUSC 2110, Introduction to Music Literature, 2 credit hours
- MUTR/THEA 2410, Movement for the Actor, 2 credit hours
- MUTR/THEA 2420, Voice Production for the Actor, 2 credit hours
- MUTR 3210, Musical Theatre I, 3 credit hours
- MUTR 3220, Musical Theatre II, 3 credit hours
- MUPF 4910, Seminar in Audition Techniques, 2 credit hours
- THEA 2110, Acting I, 3 credit hours
- THEA 2120, Acting II, 3 credit hours
- THEA 2210, Stagecraft, 3 credit hours
- THEA 2230, Special Topics in Theatre, 3 credit hours
- THEA 3110, Acting III, 3 credit hours

## 3 credit hours from:

- MUSC 3120, History of Music: Baroque/Classic, 3 credit hours
- MUSC 3130, History of Music: 19th &amp; 20th Century, 3 credit hours

4 credit hours from:

- MUTR/MUPF 1170, Intro to Musical Theatre Performance, 2 credit hours

## 6 credit hours

- THEA 2890, Production Practicum, 1 credit hour

## 8 credit hours

- MUPF 2700, Voice, 1 credit hour OR
- MUPF 4700, Voice, 1 credit hour

## 2 credit hours from:

- MUPF 1050, Keyboard Class I, 2 credit hours

## 2 credit hours from:

- DANC 1420, Ballet I, 2 credit hour
- DANC 2420, Ballet II, 2 credit hour
- DANC 3420, Ballet III, 2 credit hour

## 1 credit hours from:

- DANC 1320, Jazz I, 1 credit hour
- DANC 2320, Jazz II, 1 credit hour
- DANC 3320, Jazz III, 1 credit hour

## 1 credit hours from:

- DANC 1120, Tap I, 1 credit hour
- DANC 2120, Tap II, 1 credit hour
- DANC 3120, Tap III, 1 credit hour

## 1 credit hours from:

- DANC 2110, Musical Theatre Dance Forms, 1 credit hour

NOTE: All students must complete 120 total credit hours to graduate from Anderson University
