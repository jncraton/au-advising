## Major Requirements:

1. A plan of study must be approved by the General Studies academic advisor
2. A concentration area of 15-18 hours must be completed (with 2.0 GPA in concentration area)
3. Must complete at least 30 hours of the Liberal Arts core curriculum requirements from the following categories: ENGL 1100 or 1110, ENGL 1120, COMM 1000, Quantitative Reasoning, BIBL 2000, Personal Wellness, Scientific Ways of Knowing (WoK), Civic WoK, Aesthetic WoK and Social and Behavioral WoK
4. Students must complete 60 hours overall (with 2.0 GPA overall) and also meet other graduation requirements as listed in the catalog
