## Dance Complementary Major (33 credit hours)

## Major Requirements:

## 13 hours in dance technique:

## 3 credit hours from Global Dance Technique:

- DANC 2130, World Dance Forms, 1 credit hour
- DANC 3510, Period Dance, 2 credit hours

## 2 credit hours from Modern Technique

- DANC 1220, Modern Dance I, 1 credit hour
- DANC 2220, Modern Dance II, 1 credit hour
- DANC 3220, Modern Dance III, 1 credit hour
- DANC 4220, Modern Dance IV, 1 credit hour

## 2 credit hours from Jazz Technique

- DANC 1320, Jazz Dance I, 1 credit hour
- DANC 2320, Jazz Dance II, 1 credit hour
- DANC 3320, Jazz Dance III, 1 credit hour
- DANC 4320, Jazz Dance IV, 1 credit hour

## 2 credit hours from Ballet Technique

- DANC 1420, Ballet I, 1 credit hour
- DANC 2420, Ballet II, 1 credit hour
- DANC 3420, Ballet III, 1 credit hour
- DANC 4420, Ballet IV, 1 credit hour

## 4 credit hours and 4 semesters in:

- DANC 1150, Dance Repertory, 0-1 credit hours (arranged)

## 8 hours in dance theory from:

- DANC 3000, Dance in the Global Community, 3 credit hours

## or

- DANC 3010, Dance History Survey, 3 credit hours
- DANC 3050, Dance Pedagogy, 3 credit hours

or

- DANC 3060, Movement Analysis, 3 credit hours
- DANC 1580, Dance Improvisation I, 1 credit hour
- DANC 1590, Dance Composition I, 1 credit hour
