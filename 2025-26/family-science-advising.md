The Family Science program is designed to prepare students for professional careers in a variety of human service areas, including family services and youth programs; family life or parent education programs; business and government agencies; and leadership roles in church, school, and community.  Anderson University's Family Science program is certified by the National Council on Family Relations. Graduates of the program are eligible to apply for Provisional Certified Family Life Education (CFLE) certification.
