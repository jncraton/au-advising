## Mechanical Engineering Major Bachelor of Science (92 credit hours)

Students in Mechanical Engineering will learn the principles and skills necessary to understand how heat and mechanical power can be used in the design and operation of machines and other tools. Graduates of the Mechanical Engineering program will have a diverse background, with skills that can be ready for employment in industries such as automotive, aerospace, manufacturing, and consumer goods.
